⚖️ Customary vs Metric Units

An interactive lesson — pour, compare, and discover!

📐 Quick Reference Guide

🇺🇸 Customary Length

1 foot (ft)= 12 inches (in)
1 yard (yd)= 3 feet = 36 inches
1 mile (mi)= 1,760 yards = 5,280 ft

🌍 Metric Length

1 cm= 10 millimeters (mm)
1 decimeter (dm)= 10 cm
1 meter (m)= 100 cm = 10 dm
1 kilometer (km)= 1,000 meters

🇺🇸 Customary Capacity

1 pint (pt)= 2 cups
1 quart (qt)= 2 pints = 4 cups
1 gallon (gal)= 4 quarts = 16 cups

🌍 Metric Capacity

1 liter (L)= 1,000 milliliters (mL)
10 deciliters (dL)= 1 liter

🇺🇸 Customary Weight

1 pound (lb)= 16 ounces (oz)
1 ton (T)= 2,000 pounds

🌍 Metric Mass

1 gram (g)= 1,000 milligrams (mg)
1 kilogram (kg)= 1,000 grams

🔁 Key Conversions

1 inch≈ 2.54 cm
1 meter≈ 39.37 inches ≈ 1.09 yards
1 mile≈ 1.6 km
1 liter≈ 1.06 quarts
1 gallon≈ 3.8 liters
1 ounce≈ 28 grams
1 kilogram≈ 2.2 pounds
